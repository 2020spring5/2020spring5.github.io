Netiquette For Social Media

Be kind!

~If you wouldn't say it to their face, don't say it~

Privacy

~Don’t post any personal information such as your address, full name, phone number, or credit card digits~

Password

~Don’t share your password with anyone~

Friends

~Decline any friend requests from people you don’t know and only accept your friends'~

Think

~Think before posting anything (this includes pictures, videos, or comments)~

Report

~Report any cyberbullying~
