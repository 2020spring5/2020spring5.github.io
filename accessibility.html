What Is Web Accessibility?

Computer accessibility refers to the accessibility of a computer system to all people, regardless of disability type or severity of impairment

What Is Color Blindess?

Color blindness occurs when you are unable to see colors in a normal way. It is also known as color deficiency. Color blindness often happens when someone cannot distinguish between certain colors

Types Of Color Blindness

There are multiple times of color blindness, the follwowing are the most common ones:

  • Protanopia red-green blindness
  • Deutanopia red-green blindness
  • Tritanopia blue-yellow blindness
  • Protanomaly
  • Deutanomaly
  • Tritanomaly
  • Atypical achromatopsia
